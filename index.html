Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs was a visionary entrepreneur and co-founder of Apple Inc. He revolutionized the tech industry with iconic products like the iPhone, iPod, and MacBook. His passion for design, innovation, and simplicity reshaped how we interact with technology.

Why I Admire Him

  • He had an extraordinary vision for the future of technology.
  • He never gave up, even when things were hard.
  • He believed in his ideas and followed his dreams.
